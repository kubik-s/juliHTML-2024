A consultation usually involves a single meeting with a psychologist. Its objective is an in-depth and solution-centered evaluation of the problem.

Do not hesitate to contact us if you are dealing with any of these issues:

– personal or professional crises

– relationship crises

– bereavement

– irritability and impatience

– exhaustion, drowsiness

– anxiety and stress

– low confidence and self-esteem

– culture shock and homesickness

– incomprehensible emotions and reactions

– compulsive behaviour

– sleep problems

– difficult choices

– sexual issues

– addiction

You may be suffering, lost or may simply want to understand yourself better. In any case, if you feel that you need change and improvement in your life, but you are not sure about what measures you should take, book a consultation. I can help you to understand the situation.
